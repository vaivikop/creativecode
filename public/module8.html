Your final project is an opportunity to express your artistic voice and technical prowess. What story do you want to tell? What emotions do you want to evoke?

  • Experiment with different frameworks, libraries, and tools to enhance your project.

Remember, challenges are opportunities for growth. Embrace the process and don't be afraid to iterate and experiment!
